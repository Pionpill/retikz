---
title: Placement
description: Three ways to specify a position — Cartesian / Polar / At-relative
---

`placement` covers the three shapes that can fill a `position` field:

- [`Position`](/core/reference/placement/position) — Cartesian `[x, y]` (tuple)
- [`PolarPosition`](/core/reference/placement/polar-position) — angle + radius around an origin; recursive (origin may be another polar)
- [`AtPosition`](/core/reference/placement/at-position) — relative to another node by direction + distance

All three resolve to Cartesian at compile time.
